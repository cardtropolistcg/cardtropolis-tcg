Who We Are

Brand at a Glance

Cardtropolis is a family-run card shop with a comic-book, night-in-the-city personality. The identity mixes bold comic type, hand-lettered energy, and a glowing skyline to feel welcoming, local, and fun for players of every age.

Identity

Logo

The primary mark is a night-sky badge with the city skyline — the shop's namesake. It pairs with the hand-lettered wordmark. Use the badge on its own where space is tight (favicons, social avatars, app tiles).

Do
  • Keep clear space around the badge equal to the yellow border width.
  • Use the badge alone at small sizes; add the wordmark when there's room.
  • Place on navy, paper, or white backgrounds.
  • Keep the badge circular and unmodified.
Don't
  • Stretch, rotate, or squish the badge.
  • Recolor the skyline or swap the yellow border.
  • Add drop shadows, gradients, or glows.
  • Place the red wordmark on busy or low-contrast photos.
