Beyond the Weekly Lineup

Leagues & Special Events

Pokémon League

An official Pokémon League, Saturdays at 11 AM. Play with your own cards or video games, trade with other trainers, and earn prizes. Well suited to kids, families, and new players.

Pokémon Trade Nights

Free, all-ages trade nights that regularly bring more than 50 collectors into the shop. Bring your binder.

Release Events

New-set release events, Commander parties, and One Piece events whenever a major set launches.

Beyblade League

Multiple Beyblade league meets each month — one of our most popular events for younger players.

Days and times change month to month. The full special-events calendar is posted on our Facebook page at the start of each month.
