Who We Are

Our Story

We're a family-run card shop in Silverdale, Washington, specializing in trading card games — Pokémon, Magic: The Gathering, Yu-Gi-Oh!, Cardfight Vanguard, and newer titles like One Piece, Disney Lorcana, and Riftbound.

More than a store, Cardtropolis is a place to play. We host events every day of the week, an official Pokémon League, and Friday Night Magic, plus trade nights and release events that regularly fill the shop. Whether you're hunting a specific card, learning your first game, or bringing the kids to Beyblade league, there's a seat at our tables for you.

Can't make it in? Our full inventory of singles and sealed product is available online through our TCGplayer Pro store, with free shipping on qualifying orders.
